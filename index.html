Tips to Reduce the Carbon Footprint of Your Email

The Perfect Info

Check your emails thoroughly before sending them to ensure they contain all the necessary (and correct) information.

It avoids the need for a follow-up email.

The Correct List

Regularly clean and maintain mailing lists.

Remove any contacts that unsubscribe, and update changed email address immediately.

The Right Content

Hyperlink files or information held online rather than adding an attachment.

An email with an attachment releases 50g of CO2, against 4g for one without.
